Wyoming Test Fixtures has nearly 30 years experience designing and fabricating mechanical test fixtures of the highest quality at the lowest possible prices.

All fixtures are fabricated in a precision machine shop and inspected against the highest possible standards by experienced personnel.
WTF is an active participant in various standardization groups such as ASTM and CMH-17 and has advised in the creation of new testing standards.
WTF has many prior years of industry and university research experience in the testing and analysis of materials, particularly composite materials.
